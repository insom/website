Python Snippets: Decoding Barcodes & Framing Thumbnails

barcode.py

barcode.py is a partially complete, kind of functional barcode decoder. Commented badly and written worse, viewing the source is both advised and discouraged. (Requires PIL).

Typical USage:

Download the sample barcode, and place them all in the same directory. For extra coolness, drop in niamh.py from the verifying credit card numbers hack.

frame.py

frame.py is yet-another-thumbail-maker, which adds Tao of Mac style borders to images - though in a much less clever way - by using a pregenerated image with the border in. This means that you can tweak or replace the border easily, though. (Requires PIL, and photo-frame.bmp)
